## VORTEX COMMAND
3D Tempest remix.

https://omegeth.com/cortex-command.html

**Visuals** — Full Three.js 3D perspective tube with proper neon glow via additive blending (fake bloom that actually works). Starfield background, pulsing center-star at the inner end, lane highlight strip under your claw. Camera shake on death because pain deserves punctuation.

**10 levels** — Circle → Star → Grid → Cross → Cyclone → Nova → Fractal → Nexus → Diamond → Inferno. Each has its own color scheme, background tint, and speed multiplier. Tube spins dramatically on level clear.

**Enemy types:**
- **Flipper** (cyan) — flips between lanes as it approaches
- **Tanker** (orange) — splits into 2 Flippers on death
- **Fuseball** (magenta) — moves erratically, high points
- **Spiker** (green) — shows up at higher levels

**Game feel stuff** — combo multiplier (up to 8x for rapid kills), extra life at 10k and every 20k after, hi-score persisted in localStorage, touch controls auto-appear on mobile.

**Controls:** `← →` move, `SPACE/X` fire (hold for autofire), `Z` superzapper (kills everything on screen, 2 per level), `P` pause.
